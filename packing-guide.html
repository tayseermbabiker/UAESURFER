Money-Saving Pro Tips

  • Buy toiletries locally — major brands available but pricey
  • Get local SIM at airport — international roaming is expensive
  • Book attractions in advance — saves time and money
  • Hotels add 22% in taxes plus Tourism Dirham fee (AED 7-20/night)
  • Haggle in souks — initial prices are often 3-4x fair price
  • Pack light, modest, and smart — you can buy almost anything in Dubai's malls
